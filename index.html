About

I am a New York native, born and raised on Long Island. I earned my undergraduate degree at Queens College and my J.D. from Hofstra University School of Law and have been a practicing attorney since 2010.

Upon graduating from law school, I worked for a boutique plaintiff’s mass torts firm specializing in defective drugs and medical devices. After practicing there for 5 years, I joined Morgan & Morgan to help grow our firm by helping to open our New York office and bring a fresh perspective on new technologies and processes in the legal field. I am admitted to practice in New York, including the United States District Court for the Eastern District of New York.

Our firm practices solely on contingency. We have an extensive employee rights division, focusing on helping people who have been denied their pay, have been harassed or discriminated against, suffered a wrongful termination or have been denied to be hired. We also have a Business Trial Group which handles contract disputes for individuals and businesses. We are proud of the work that we do and of our ability to make legal help accesible to everyone.

Together with my wife Miri we have 3 children - 2 girls and 1 boy. In my free time I am an avid cyclist and passionate DIYer.
